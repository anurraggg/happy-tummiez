# Privacy Policy for HappyTummy

**Last Updated: December 3, 2025**

## Introduction
Welcome to HappyTummy. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information**: Name, email address when you sign up
- **Quiz Responses**: Your answers to the Digestive Quotient test
- **Game Scores**: Your performance in our interactive games

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data**: Pages visited, features used, time spent
- **Device Information**: Browser type, device type, IP address
- **Cookies**: We use cookies to improve your experience

## How We Use Your Information
- Provide and improve our services
- Personalize your experience
- Analyze usage patterns
- Send important updates (with your consent)
- Ensure security and prevent fraud

## Google Analytics
We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our site. Google Analytics collects:
- Pages you visit
- Time spent on pages
- Your general location (city/country)
- Device and browser information

You can opt-out of Google Analytics by installing the [Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
